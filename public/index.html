Our Mission

Our mission is to empower developers and designers with cutting-edge, visually stunning components. We believe that interaction should be seamless, and presentation should be unforgettable. The 3D Coverflow was born from the desire to modernize the classic gallery view.

From Idea to Reality

Founded in 2024, 3D Coverflow began as a small passion project focused on CSS-only 3D transformations. It quickly evolved into a robust, framework-agnostic library used by thousands of websites worldwide. We pride ourselves on clean code, extensive documentation, and an active community.

The Team

We are a globally distributed team of creative developers, UX experts, and performance engineers. We are united by a passion for pushing the limits of what's possible on the modern web. We continually update the library based on community feedback and the latest web standards.
